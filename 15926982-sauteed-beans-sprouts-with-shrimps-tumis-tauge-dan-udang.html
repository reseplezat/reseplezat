Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat SautÃ©ed Beans Sprouts with Shrimps (Tumis Tauge dan Udang):

  1. 7 peeled shrimps (7 ekor udang ukuran besar)
  2. 1/4 teaspoon salt (1/4 sendok teh garam)
  3. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper (1/4 sendok teh bubuk lada hitam)
  4. 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped (2 siung bawang putih, rajang halus)
  5. 2 teaspoon lemon juice (2 sendok teh perasan lemon)
  6. For beans sprouts
  7. 150 grams beans sprouts (150 gram tauge atau kecambah)
  8. 2 cloves shallots, thinly sliced (2 siung bawang merah, iris halus)
  9. 3 garlic, finely chopped (3 siung bawang putih, rajang halus)
  10. 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger (1/2 sendok teh jahe parut)
  11. 1 teaspoon soy sauce (1 sendok teh kecap asin)
  12. 1 teaspoon fish sauce (1 sendok teh kecap ikan)
  13. 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (1 sendok teh saus tiram)
  14. 1/4 teaspoon white ground pepper (1/4 sendok teh bubuk lada putih)
  15. 1/2 teaspoon mushroom seasoning powder (1/2 sendok teh bubuk kaldu jamur)
  16. 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste (1/4 sendok teh garam, sesuaikan)
  17. 1/4 teaspoon chili pepper flakes (1/4 sendok teh cabai kering kasar)
  18. 1 scallion (1buah daun bawang)
  19. 10 fresh cilantro leaves, finely chopped (10 lembar daun ketumbar segar, rajang halus)

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat SautÃ©ed Beans Sprouts with Shrimps (Tumis Tauge dan Udang)

1
Heat the medium frying pan on small to medium heat. Pour 2 table spoon olive oil. (Panaskan 2 sendok minyak zaitun dalam penggorengan ukuran sedang)
2
Once the oil begins to simmer then add the shrimps. Cook about 2-3 minutes. When their color turn into pink on the bottom, quickly flip them over. Finish cooking for another 1-2 minutes until they are pink and loosely curled. Put aside. (ketika minyak sudah panas, masukkan udang dan masak selama 2-3 menit sampai warna pink bagian bawah udang, balik dan masak 1-2 menit. Sisihkan)
3
In the same pan, add 2 more tablespoon olive oil. SautÃ© shallots, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. (Dalam penggorengan yang sama, tuang 2 sendok makan minyak zaitun, tumis bawang merah, bawang putih dan jahe sampai wangi)
4
Then add in soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, white ground pepper, mushroom seasoning powder, salt, chiller pepper flakes and scallion. (Masukkan kecap asin, kecap ikan, saus tiram, bubuk lada putih, bubuk kaldu jamur, garam, bubuk cabai kasar, dan daun bawang)
5
Turn heat into high then add in the beans sprouts and stir quickly for 30 seconds. Reduced heat, then add in shrimp, and cilantro leaves. Mix for another 10 second. (Besarkan api kompor, aduk dan masak tauge kurang lebih 30 detik, kecilkan api, masukkan udang dan daun ketumbar, aduk selama 10 detik. Matikan kompor)
6
It is best to serve with hot steamed rice. EnjoyğŸ˜‹ (lebih enak disantap dengan nasi panas. Selamat MakanğŸ˜‹)
